About Me

I am Harshvardhan Takawale, a third year computer science PhD student at the University of Maryland College Park working at iCoSMoS Lab with Prof. Nirupam Roy. My research envisions to enable advanced Acoustic and RF sensing and inference on wearable and low-power computing platforms embedded in everyday objects and environments. It aims to introduce new capabilities to these resource-constrained and intermittently-powered systems perceive advanced features for interactions through gestures, micro-motions, directions, and physical contexts for various environment sensing applications.

Recently, I interned at Nokia Bell Labs UK in the Pervasive Systems group on detecting audiotory attention on earables. In the past, I have worked as a Lead Researcher at Silence Laboratories Singapore. I finished my B.E at BITS Pilani in August 2020.


Research interests: Wearable sensing, Acoustics, Multimodal Imaging, Physics-informed Machine Learning, Ubiquitous Healthcare
